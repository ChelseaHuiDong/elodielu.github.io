About Me

If I were to describe myself in one word, I would pick international. Being a Chinese girl, I studied North American Accounting, both IRFS and GAAP, in college. After that I went to France for my first master degree in International Accounting and Finance. Thanks to this amazing year in paradise-like Cote d’Azure, I also got the chance to travel around Europe and learnt snowboarding in Mont Alps. Now I am in Minneapolis MN,US, paying my debt to the snow queen.

Inspired by my consulting internship in Shanghai, I saw the business world’s craving for data-driven decisions and I myself am also much more comfortable giving my recommendations backed by data. Therefore, I am on my second master degree in Business Analytics, connecting business, statistics and machine learning!
